## Data Collection Strategy

For each company, we will collect:

1. **SEC Filings** (Past 3-4 years)
   - 10-K Annual Reports
   - 10-Q Quarterly Reports
   - 8-K Current Reports (major events)

2. **Earnings Call Transcripts** (Past 12 quarters)
   - Q1-Q4 for each fiscal year
   - Focus on management commentary on CapEx and strategic investments

3. **Investor Presentations** (Past 3 years)
   - Quarterly earnings presentations
   - Investor day presentations
   - Industry conference presentations

4. **Press Releases and News** (Past 3 years)
   - Major CapEx announcements
   - Facility expansions
   - M&A activities
   - Product launches related to AI/data center

---

## Key Metrics to Extract

1. **Capital Expenditure (CapEx)**
   - Total CapEx by quarter/year
   - CapEx as % of revenue
   - CapEx breakdown by business segment (if available)

2. **Business Segment Revenue**
   - AI/Data Center related revenue
   - Traditional business revenue
   - Growth rates for each segment

3. **Strategic Initiatives**
   - Acquisitions and divestitures
   - Facility expansions
   - Technology partnerships
   - R&D investments

4. **Management Commentary**
   - Forward-looking statements on CapEx
   - Strategic priorities
   - Market opportunity assessments
   - Risk factors

---

## Analysis Objectives

1. **Quantify Competitor Focus**: Determine the proportion of investment flowing to "AI/Data Center" vs. "Traditional" sectors
2. **Identify Risk Exposure**: Assess collective industry overexposure to AI spending trends
3. **Benchmark Against Flex**: Compare competitors' strategies with Flex's positioning
4. **Inform Strategic Decisions**: Provide actionable insights for Flex's investment strategy
