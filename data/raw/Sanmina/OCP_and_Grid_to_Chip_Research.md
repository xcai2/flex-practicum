     - Height: 2,286 mm (90 in)
     - Width: 600 mm (23.62 in)
     - Depth: 1,068 mm (42.05 in)
     - Load Rating: 1,400 kg (3,080 lbs)
     - Heavy Duty Casters
     - 21-inch OU mounting

     - DC ORv3 Busbar (48V DC up to 18 kW)

     - Tool-free mounting
     - Cable pass-through on top and bottom of rack
     - Flexible to adapt rack to customer needs under OCP compliance
