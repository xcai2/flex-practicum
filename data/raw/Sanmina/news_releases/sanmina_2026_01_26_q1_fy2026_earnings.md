## Summary

Sanmina reported spectacular Q1 FY2026 results with revenue of $3.19 billion (up 59% YoY) and non-GAAP EPS of $2.38 (up 65% YoY), driven by strong demand in Communications Networks and Cloud & AI Infrastructure end-markets. The ZT Systems integration is proceeding as expected, positioning Sanmina for continued growth in AI infrastructure.

---

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q1 FY2026 Results (Quarter Ended December 27, 2025)

**Revenue and Profitability:**
- **Revenue:** $3.19 billion (up 59% YoY from $2.01 billion)
- **GAAP Operating Margin:** 2.3%
- **Non-GAAP Operating Margin:** 6.0%
- **GAAP Diluted EPS:** $0.89
- **Non-GAAP Diluted EPS:** $2.38 (up 65% YoY from $1.44)

**Cash Flow and Balance Sheet:**
- **Cash Flow from Operations:** $179 million
- **Free Cash Flow:** $92 million
- **Share Repurchases:** 516 thousand shares for $79 million
- **Ending Cash:** $1.42 billion

**Performance vs. Guidance:**
- Revenue and non-GAAP operating margin at high-end of outlook
- Non-GAAP EPS exceeded outlook ($2.38 vs. $1.95-$2.25 range)
- Solid cash flow from operations
