# Sanmina Reports Fourth Quarter and Fiscal 2025 Financial Results

**Date:** November 3, 2025  
**Type:** Earnings Release / Financial Results

## Summary

Sanmina reported strong Q4 and full-year fiscal 2025 results, with revenue increasing 7.4% for the fiscal year driven by strength in Communications Networks and Cloud and AI Infrastructure end-markets. The company delivered non-GAAP operating margin expansion of 30 basis points and non-GAAP diluted EPS growth of 14.4%. Looking ahead to fiscal 2026, Sanmina's acquisition of ZT Systems is transformative, expected to significantly increase scale and expand capabilities in the Cloud and AI end-market.

---

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q4 Fiscal 2025 Results (Quarter Ended September 27, 2025)

**GAAP Results:**
- **Revenue:** $2.1 billion
- **GAAP Operating Margin:** 3.7%
- **GAAP Diluted EPS:** $0.88

**Non-GAAP Results:**
- **Non-GAAP Operating Margin:** 6.0%
- **Non-GAAP Diluted EPS:** $1.67

**Cash Flow:**
- **Cash Flow from Operations:** $199 million
- **Free Cash Flow:** $137 million
- **Ending Cash and Cash Equivalents:** $926 million

### Full-Year Fiscal 2025 Results (Year Ended September 27, 2025)

**GAAP Results:**
- **Revenue:** $8.1 billion (up 7.4% YoY)
- **GAAP Operating Margin:** 4.4%
- **GAAP Diluted EPS:** $4.46

**Non-GAAP Results:**
- **Non-GAAP Operating Margin:** 5.7% (expanded 30 basis points)
- **Non-GAAP Diluted EPS:** $6.04 (up 14.4% YoY)

**Cash Flow and Capital Allocation:**
- **Cash Flow from Operations:** $621 million
- **Free Cash Flow:** $478 million
- **Share Repurchases:** 1.44 million shares for $114 million

**About Sanmina:**

Sanmina Corporation, a Fortune 500 company, is a leading integrated manufacturing solutions provider serving the fastest growing segments of the global Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) market. Recognized as a technology leader, Sanmina provides end-to-end manufacturing solutions, delivering superior quality and support to Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) primarily in the industrial, medical, defense and aerospace, automotive, communications networks and cloud and AI infrastructure markets. Sanmina has facilities strategically located in key regions throughout the world.
