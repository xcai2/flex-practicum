# Benchmark Breaks Ground on its 5th Facility in Penang

**Date:** February 17, 2025  
**Type:** Facility Expansion / CapEx Investment  
**Source:** https://ir.bench.com/news/news-details/2025/Benchmark-Breaks-Ground-on-its-5th-Facility-in-Penang/default.aspx

**Tags:** CapEx, facility expansion, semiconductor, WFE, Malaysia, Penang  
**Relevance:** HIGH - Semiconductor capital equipment for AI chip manufacturing  
**CapEx Related:** Yes  
**AI Related:** Indirect (semiconductor manufacturing equipment for AI chips)

---

## Summary

Benchmark held a groundbreaking ceremony for its 5th facility in Penang, Malaysia, with an initial investment of USD $25 million (RM 110 million). The 215,000-square-foot facility will focus on Wafer Fab Equipment (WFE) manufacturing for semiconductor capital equipment customers, with completion expected by June 2026.

---

## Facility Details

### Investment and Size
- **Initial Investment:** USD $25 million (RM 110 million)
- **Facility Size:** 215,000 square feet (two-story building)
- **Total Penang Footprint:** 720,000+ square feet (after completion)
- **Construction Timeline:** 18 months (Feb 2025 - June 2026)
- **Location:** Batu Kawan Industrial Park, next to existing Benchmark Penang Cassia facility

### Facility Features
- **Office Space:** Administrative and engineering areas
- **Production Area:** Manufacturing floor with specialized capabilities
- **Cleanrooms:** Controlled environment for semiconductor equipment assembly
- **Complex Machining:** Precision machining capabilities
- **Powder Painting:** State-of-the-art automated powder coat line
- **Multi-Story Car Park:** Employee parking facilities
- **Large-Form-Factor Machining:** Space for oversized precision components

## Analysis Notes

This facility expansion is strategically important for Benchmark's semiconductor capital equipment business, which indirectly supports AI chip manufacturing. While not a direct AI data center investment, WFE manufacturing is critical infrastructure for the AI supply chain.

**AI Supply Chain Connection:**
1. **AI Chips:** GPUs, TPUs, custom AI processors require advanced fabrication
2. **Fab Equipment:** WFE (lithography, etching, deposition, etc.) manufactures AI chips
3. **Benchmark:** Manufactures complex WFE modules and systems for equipment OEMs
4. **Indirect AI Exposure:** Benefits from AI chip demand driving WFE investments
