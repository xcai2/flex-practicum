# Benchmark Opens Advanced Manufacturing Facility in Guadalajara

**Date:** June 25, 2025  
**Type:** Facility Expansion / CapEx Investment

## Summary

Benchmark officially inaugurated its new advanced manufacturing facility in Guadalajara, Mexico, representing a 50% expansion in the company's manufacturing footprint in the region. The 321,000-square-foot facility is designed to serve OEMs in medical technologies, industrial applications, next-gen communications, and advanced computing sectors.

---

## Facility Details

### Location and Size
- **Location:** Parque Industrial San Jorge, Tlajomulco, Guadalajara, Mexico
- **Size:** 321,000 square feet
- **Expansion:** 50% increase in Benchmark's regional manufacturing footprint
- **Opening Date:** June 25, 2025 (grand opening ceremony)

### Target Markets
1. **Medical Technologies** - AEDs, tissue oximetry, radiotherapy power systems
2. **Industrial Applications** - Oil & gas flow meters, telematics, HVAC controls
3. **Next-Gen Communications** - Satellite broadband, VoIP infrastructure, smart city hardware
4. **Advanced Computing** - High-performance computing systems
