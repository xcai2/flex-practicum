# SANMINA ANNOUNCES ACQUISITION OF DATA CENTER INFRASTRUCTURE MANUFACTURING BUSINESS OF ZT SYSTEMS FROM AMD

**Type:** Major Acquisition / Strategic Investment  
**Source:** https://ir.sanmina.com/news/news-details/2025/SANMINA-ANNOUNCES-ACQUISITION-OF-DATA-CENTER-INFRASTRUCTURE-MANUFACTURING-BUSINESS-OF-ZT-SYSTEMS-FROM-AMD/default.aspx

**Relevance:** VERY HIGH - Transformational $3B acquisition, AI infrastructure focus

## Summary

Sanmina announced a definitive agreement to acquire the data center infrastructure manufacturing business of ZT Systems from AMD for up to $3 billion ($2.55 billion upfront + $450 million contingent). This transformational acquisition positions Sanmina as an industry leader in Cloud and AI infrastructure manufacturing, with ZT Systems bringing $5-6 billion annual revenue run-rate, advanced liquid cooling capabilities, and deep hyperscaler relationships.

---

## Transaction Details

### Purchase Price

**Total: Up to $3.0 Billion**
- **$2.25 Billion Cash:** For assets at target net asset value (with adjustment mechanism)
- **$300 Million Premium:** 50% cash / 50% equity
- **$450 Million Contingent:** Based on financial performance over next 3 years
- **Total Upfront:** $2.55 billion (cash + equity)

### Financing

- **Committed Financing:** $2.5 billion from Bank of America
- **Equity Component:** AMD receives Sanmina equity with 3-year lock-up
  - Year 1: Up to 33% can be sold
  - Year 2: Up to 33% can be sold
  - Year 3: Remaining equity can be sold

### Timeline

- **Announcement:** May 19, 2025
- **Expected Close:** End of 2025 calendar year
- **Completion:** October 27, 2025 (completed ahead of schedule)
- **Conditions:** Regulatory approvals and customary closing conditions

## Strategic Rationale

### 1. Increased Scale and High-Value End-Market Exposure

The addition of ZT Systems' manufacturing business significantly enhances Sanmina's position in the fast-growing Cloud and AI end-market. It is expected to **double Sanmina's revenue scale in three years**.

**Market Opportunity:**
- Cloud infrastructure market growing rapidly
- AI infrastructure driving unprecedented demand
- Hyperscaler CapEx increasing for AI data centers
- Liquid cooling becoming standard for AI systems

### 2. Broadens and Deepens Cloud and AI Hyperscaler Customer Relationships

Through this transaction, Sanmina is well-positioned to both expand and deepen relationships with existing customers by offering a more comprehensive, integrated solution from a trusted partner, while also adding new customers through ZT Systems' relationships with hyperscalers.

**Customer Benefits:**
- End-to-end manufacturing solutions
- Vertical integration capabilities
- U.S. and European manufacturing capacity
- Advanced liquid cooling expertise
- Accelerated time-to-market

### 3. Brings Industry-Leading Manufacturing Capacity and Know-How

ZT Systems brings large state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities located in New Jersey and Texas, with advanced liquid cooling capabilities, that are complementary to Sanmina's existing domestic manufacturing footprint. ZT Systems also operates a facility in the Netherlands, offering the opportunity to expand Sanmina's presence in Europe.

**Geographic Expansion:**
- **U.S. Capacity:** New Jersey and Texas facilities
- **European Presence:** Netherlands facility
- **Complementary:** Adds to Sanmina's existing global footprint
- **Strategic Locations:** Close to hyperscaler customers

### 4. Reinforces Capabilities as Leading End-To-End Manufacturing Partner

The combination enhances Sanmina's position as an industry leader and bolsters the Company's opportunities to capitalize on the increased demand for digital infrastructure. The addition of ZT Systems' manufacturing capabilities to Sanmina's global portfolio, mission-critical technologies and vertical integration enables solutions for the entire product lifecycle: design, engineering, manufacturing and fulfillment.

**End-to-End Solutions:**
- Design and engineering
- Component manufacturing
- Systems integration
- Complete rack assembly
- Fulfillment and logistics

### 5. Enhances Ability to Support Broad Customer Base in Cloud and AI

The addition of ZT Systems' manufacturing business establishes Sanmina's position in the industry, creating an all-encompassing offering with the addition of full systems integration at scale. Sanmina will now have the capabilities needed to partner closely with both hyperscaler and OEM customers across all platforms and technologies in the industry.

**Customer Segments:**
- **Hyperscalers:** Direct relationships with largest cloud providers
- **OEMs:** AMD and other AI infrastructure vendors
- **Cloud Providers:** Regional and specialized cloud services
- **Enterprise:** Large-scale private cloud deployments

### 6. Expected to Be Accretive to Earnings

The acquisition of ZT Systems' manufacturing business is expected to be accretive to Sanmina's non-GAAP EPS in the first year after closing, with further EPS accretion expected as synergies are fully realized over time.

**Financial Benefits:**
- First-year EPS accretion
- Operational synergies from scale
- Vertical integration efficiencies
- Revenue growth from market expansion

### 7. Compelling Synergy Opportunities

The combination is expected to generate synergies, driven primarily by operational efficiencies due to scale and vertical integration.

**Synergy Sources:**
- Purchasing power and supply chain optimization
- Manufacturing efficiency and capacity utilization
- Shared infrastructure and overhead
- Technology and process best practices

## Strategic Partnership with AMD

AMD will retain ZT Systems' AI systems design businesses and will work with Sanmina as a U.S.-based new product introduction (NPI) manufacturing partner of choice to accelerate quality and time-to-deployment of AMD AI rack and cluster-scale systems for cloud customers.

**Partnership Benefits:**
- **Preferred NPI Partner:** Sanmina becomes AMD's preferred U.S.-based NPI manufacturing partner
- **AMD AI Solutions:** Accelerates U.S.-based manufacturing of AMD AI training and inference solutions
- **Time-to-Market:** Faster deployment of AMD AI systems for cloud customers
- **Quality and Flexibility:** Sets standard for quality and flexibility in AI ecosystem
- **Open Approach:** Based on AMD's open approach to AI infrastructure

**AMD Product Focus:**
- AI rack and cluster-scale systems
- End-to-end training solutions
- Inference solutions
- Optimized for customer unique environments
- Ready-to-deploy at scale

### Liquid Cooling for AI

Liquid cooling has become essential for AI infrastructure due to extreme power density of GPU-accelerated servers. ZT Systems' advanced liquid cooling capabilities position Sanmina to serve this high-growth market.

**Liquid Cooling Benefits:**
- Higher power density support (100+ kW per rack)
- Better thermal performance for sustained GPU performance
- Energy efficiency (lower PUE)
- Enables AI cluster scale and performance
