## Summary

Celestica launched the Spark Partner Program™, a new global channel initiative designed to empower distributors, value-added resellers (VARs), and systems integrators to grow profitability while delivering greater value to customers. The program provides partners with enablement tools, deal registration, demo units, and technical support to expand Celestica's reach into high-growth markets.

---

## Full Announcement

We're excited to announce the launch of the Celestica Spark Partner Program™, a new global channel initiative designed to empower our distributors, value-added resellers, and systems integrators to grow profitability while delivering greater value to customers.

The technology landscape is evolving faster than ever and our customers are on a transformation journey that demands best-in-class, flexible solutions. We recognize that channel partners are essential navigators on this journey. The Spark Partner Program was created with both partners and customers in mind.

### Program Overview

**Devann Garner, Sales Leader for Portfolio Solutions, Celestica:**
> "Channel partners are essential to our customers' transformation journey and play a pivotal role in expanding our reach into high-growth markets. As we continue to scale, this program ensures that our partners have the support, training, and go-to-market resources needed to differentiate their offerings, grow their business, and drive meaningful customer outcomes."

### Benefits for Partners

At its core, the Spark Partner Program is about simplicity, flexibility, and growth. Recognizing that partners operate under diverse business models, the program is structured to let each partner choose their engagement level and unlock exclusive benefits based on their commitment and growth achievements.

**Key Partner Benefits:**
- **Enablement Tools and Resources:** Training, documentation, and go-to-market materials
- **Deal Registration Program:** Protect partner-sourced opportunities
- **Access to Evaluation and Demo Units:** Hands-on product experience for customers
- **Pre-Sales and Technical Support:** Expert assistance throughout the sales cycle
- **Open-Source Flexibility:** Create tailored solutions and unique service offerings
- **Tiered Engagement:** Choose engagement level based on business model and goals

### Delivering Customer Value

While the Spark Partner Program is for channel partners, its impact is felt strongly by customers. By combining Celestica's tier 1 hardware platforms and the specialized, value-added services of partners, customers gain more flexibility with faster time-to-value.

**Celestica Product Portfolio:**
- **Data Center Switches:** DS6000/DS6001 (1.6TbE), DS5000 series (800G)
- **Campus Switches:** Enterprise networking solutions
- **Storage Controllers:** High-performance storage management
- **Storage Expansion Products:** SD6300 ultra-dense JBOD
- **Celestica Care Support Packages:** Technical support and maintenance

These products are engineered for high performance, scalability, and reliability, helping to address customers' needs from the data center to the edge.

## Analysis Notes

The Spark Partner Program represents Celestica's strategic move to scale its go-to-market capabilities beyond direct sales to hyperscalers. While Celestica has strong relationships with large hyperscaler customers, the channel program enables penetration of enterprise and mid-market segments where partners play a critical role.

**Strategic Rationale:**
- **Market Expansion:** Access enterprise customers who prefer to buy through channels
- **Scalability:** Leverage partner resources to reach more customers
- **Local Presence:** Partners provide local support and expertise
- **Value-Added Services:** Partners can customize and integrate solutions
- **Competitive Positioning:** Structured program competes with established vendors

**Target Customers (via Partners):**

- Traditional data center customers
- Organizations requiring open networking solutions
