## Summary

Celestica introduced two new 1.6TbE data center switches, the DS6000 and DS6001, designed to power AI/ML clusters. These switches double the capacity of existing 800G solutions and are based on the Broadcom Tomahawk 6 chipset, providing up to 102.4Tbps of switching capacity.

## Full Press Release

TORONTO, Oct. 10, 2025 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- Celestica Inc. (TSX: CLS) (NYSE: CLS), a leader in design, manufacturing, supply chain and platform solutions for the world's most innovative companies, today introduced two new 1.6TbE data center switches, the DS6000 and DS6001. These advanced switches are engineered to support high-bandwidth, AI/ML, data center applications. Celestica plans to contribute the DS6000/DS6001 spec to the OCP Community and list them on the OCP Marketplace with the OCP Inspired™ recognition.

### Product Specifications

**DS6000 (3RU Air-Cooled):**
- **Form Factor:** 3RU (3 rack units)
- **Ports:** 64-port x 1.6TbE OSFP
- **Switching Capacity:** 102.4Tbps
- **Chipset:** Broadcom Tomahawk 6 (TH6)
- **SerDes:** 224G SerDes lanes
- **Optics Support:** LPO / LRO optics
- **Port Speeds:** 50/100/200/400/800GbE capable
- **Cooling:** Traditional air-cooled
- **Redundancy:** Redundant, field-replaceable fans and hot-swappable power supplies
- **Airflow:** Forward and reverse airflow configurations
- **Target:** Traditional 19-inch data center racks

**DS6001 (2OU Hybrid-Cooled):**
- **Form Factor:** 2OU (2 Open Rack units)
- **Ports:** 64-port x 1.6TbE
- **Switching Capacity:** 102.4Tbps (same as DS6000)
- **Chipset:** Broadcom Tomahawk 6 (TH6)
- **Cooling:** Hybrid cooling (air and liquid combination)
- **Rack:** 21-inch OCP ORv3 rack
- **Density:** 2U rack density
- **Target:** OCP Open Rack v3 environments with hybrid cooling

### Key Features

**Performance:**
- 102.4Tbps switching capacity
