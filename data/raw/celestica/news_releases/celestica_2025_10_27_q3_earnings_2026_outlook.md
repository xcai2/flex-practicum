# Celestica Announces Strong Q3 2025 Results and Raises 2026 Outlook on AI Data Center Demand

**Date:** October 27, 2025  
**Type:** Earnings Release / Financial Results  
**Source:** https://corporate.celestica.com/news-releases/news-release-details/celestica-announces-third-quarter-2025-financial-results-and

**Tags:** earnings, AI, data center, financial results, growth outlook, hyperscaler  
**Relevance:** VERY HIGH - Strong AI-driven growth and 2026 outlook  
**CapEx Related:** Indirect (customer CapEx driving demand)  
**AI Related:** Yes

---

## Summary

Celestica reported strong Q3 2025 results with revenue of $3.19 billion (+28% YoY) and raised its 2025 and 2026 outlooks, driven by continued strong demand from hyperscaler customers making significant investments in AI data center infrastructure. The company projects 2026 revenue of $16.0 billion (+31% growth) with indications of strong demand continuing into 2027.

---

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q3 2025 Results

**Revenue and Profitability:**
- Revenue: $3.19 billion (+28% vs Q3 2024)
- Adjusted Operating Margin: 7.6% (vs 6.8% in Q3 2024)
- Adjusted EPS: $1.58 (+52% vs Q3 2024)
- GAAP EPS: $2.31 (vs $0.75 in Q3 2024)
- GAAP Operating Margin: 10.2% (vs 5.5% in Q3 2024)

**Segment Performance:**
- CCS Segment Revenue: $2.41 billion (+43% YoY), 8.3% margin
- Hardware Platform Solutions: ~$1.4 billion (+79% YoY)
- ATS Segment Revenue: $0.78 billion (-4% YoY), 5.5% margin

### 2025 Annual Outlook Update (Raised)

- Revenue: $12.2 billion (previous: $11.55 billion)
- Adjusted EPS: $5.90 (previous: $5.50)
- Adjusted Operating Margin: 7.4% (unchanged)
- Free Cash Flow: $425 million (previous: $400 million)

### 2026 Annual Outlook (New)

- Revenue: $16.0 billion (+31% growth)
- Adjusted EPS: $8.20 (+39% growth)
- Adjusted Operating Margin: 7.8%
- Free Cash Flow: $500 million

---

## CEO Commentary on AI Data Center Demand

**Rob Mionis, President and CEO:**

> "We achieved very strong results in the third quarter, with revenue of $3.19 billion and non-GAAP adjusted EPS of $1.58, representing growth of 28% and 52%, respectively, each exceeding the high end of our guidance ranges. In the quarter we achieved strong adjusted operating margin of 7.6%, another high for the company, reflecting the continued strength of our execution."

> "Driven by these strong results to date and a demand environment that continues to strengthen, we are pleased to increase our 2025 annual outlook. We now expect revenue to reach $12.2 billion, an increase from the prior $11.55 billion, and anticipate non-GAAP adjusted EPS of $5.90, up from our previous estimate of $5.50."

> "Furthermore, we are announcing our 2026 annual outlook with revenue of $16.0 billion and non-GAAP adjusted EPS of $8.20, representing growth of 31% and 39% respectively. **The demand outlook from our largest customers, who continue to make significant investments in AI data center infrastructure, remains strong, supporting our 2026 annual outlook with indications of these dynamics continuing into 2027.**"
