# Celestica Introduces the SD6300 Platform for AI and Enterprise Storage

**Source**: Celestica Inc. Official Press Release  
**Date**: November 17, 2025  
**URL**: https://corporate.celestica.com/news-releases/news-release-details/celestica-introduces-sd6300-platform-aiming-deliver-maximum  
**Type**: Official Company Announcement

---

## Summary

Celestica Inc. (NYSE: CLS) (TSX: CLS) announced the SD6300 ultra-dense storage expansion (JBOD) system designed to address data growth challenges in AI environments, traditional enterprise, and hyperscale data centers.

## Key Product Features

- **Form Factor**: 4U JBOD with SAS-4 uplink
- **Capacity**: 108 dual-port LFF SAS drive bays
- **Footprint**: 1125 mm (including cable management) - fits standard 1200 mm racks
- **Performance**: Up to four SAS-4 SSDs for enhanced read/write performance
- **Architecture**: High-availability/redundant architecture

## Industry Recognition

According to TRENDFOCUS (leading storage analyst firm), the SD6300 is:
- Industry's highest density ultra-dense JBOD
- Most compact ultra-dense JBOD
- Enables CapEx and OpEx savings

## Target Applications

**AI-Specific Use Cases:**
- AI data ingest phase
- AI data archiving phase

**Traditional Use Cases:**
- Data lakes

- Enterprises (research, oil & gas, government, healthcare, financial services)
