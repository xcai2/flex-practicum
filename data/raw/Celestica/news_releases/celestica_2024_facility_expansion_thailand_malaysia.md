# Celestica Expands Manufacturing Capacity in Thailand and Malaysia for AI Data Center Infrastructure

**Date:** 2024 (Ongoing through 2025-2026)  
**Type:** Facility Expansion / CapEx Investment  
**Source:** Multiple sources including 10-Q filings, earnings calls, and proxy statements

**Tags:** CapEx, facility expansion, AI, data center, manufacturing, Thailand, Malaysia  
**Relevance:** VERY HIGH - Major capacity expansion for AI data center production  
**CapEx Related:** Yes  
**AI Related:** Yes

---

## Summary

Celestica has undertaken significant capacity and capability expansions at its Thailand and Malaysia facilities to support the rapid growth in AI data center infrastructure demand from hyperscaler customers. These expansions are part of a multi-year investment program to scale manufacturing capabilities for AI servers, networking equipment, and storage solutions.

---

### Thailand Facility
- **Location:** Thailand manufacturing campus
- **Focus:** AI server manufacturing, networking equipment, storage platforms
- **Investment Type:** Capacity expansion and capability upgrades
- **Timeline:** 2024-2026 (ongoing)
- **Purpose:** Support hyperscaler AI data center infrastructure demand

### Malaysia Facility
- **Location:** Malaysia manufacturing campus
- **Focus:** Data center infrastructure components and systems
- **Investment Type:** Capacity and capability expansions
- **Timeline:** 2024-2026 (ongoing)
- **Purpose:** Scale production for AI/cloud infrastructure

### Richardson, Texas (U.S.) Facility
- **Location:** Richardson, Texas, United States
- **Focus:** Advanced technology solutions and data center infrastructure
- **Investment Type:** Capacity and capability expansions
- **Timeline:** 2024-2026 (ongoing)
- **Purpose:** Domestic manufacturing for AI data center customers
