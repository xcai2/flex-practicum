## Article Summary

This article analyzes Celestica's SD6300 product launch in the context of competitive positioning and market growth opportunities in the AI data center infrastructure market.

## Key Points

### Market Context
The AI data center market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 25.85% from 2025 to 2030 (per Mordor Intelligence report). Celestica is expanding its portfolio to capitalize on this trend.

### Competitive Landscape

**Jabil Inc. (JBL)**
- Investing $500 million over several years in Southeast U.S. region
- Focus: Expanding manufacturing capabilities for cloud and AI data center infrastructure
- Goal: Strengthen position in AI hardware supply chain

**Flex Ltd. (FLEX)**
- Aggressively moving into high-growth data center market
- Collaborating with LG and NVIDIA
- 2026 Target: ~$6.5 billion in data center revenues (35%+ YoY growth)

### Stock Performance
Celestica shares increased 256.3% over the past year, compared to industry growth of 109.1%.

### Target Markets
The SD6300 is positioned for multiple sectors including energy, healthcare, government, and financial services.
