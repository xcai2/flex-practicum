- Celestica chosen by a major hyperscaler to design rack scale networking solutions for AI data centers
- Mass production planned to begin in 2026
- Expands Celestica's role in AI data center infrastructure and networking hardware

### Stock Performance

- 1-year return: 141.6%
- 5-year return: Above 7x
- Analyst target midpoint: CA$555.29

### Valuation Assessment
- **Simply Wall St Fair Value**: Shares trading ~187% above estimated fair value (flagged as overvalued)
- **P/E Ratio**: 49.7x
- **30-day return**: 1.6% (modest recent gains)

### Strategic Implications
The hyperscaler networking mandate creates closer ties to AI data center spending, which provides:
- Longer-term visibility on future demand
- Increased sensitivity to that buyer's deployment plans
- Potential for significant volume as mass production scales in 2026
