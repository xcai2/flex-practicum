### Thermal Management Solutions
1. **Liquid Cooling Technologies**
   - Single-phase liquid cooling: circulates liquid coolant that absorbs heat without changing state
   - Dual-phase liquid cooling: leverages coolant's phase change from liquid to vapor for higher heat transfer efficiency
   - Rack-level and data center-level thermal solutions
   - Cooling Distribution Units (CDUs): both in-row and in-rack configurations
   - Cold plates and manifolds design
   - Co-design solutions with silicon partners for dense deployments
   - Advanced airflow and containment systems
   - Source: https://www.celestica.com/blog/article/ai-infrastructure-for-the-data-center-and-beyond

2. **CDU Design Expertise**
   - In-row CDUs: installed between several racks, centralized cooling for multiple racks
   - In-rack CDUs: integrated directly into individual racks, targeted cooling with smaller footprint
   - Optimized data center thermal footprints for hyperscaler customers
   - Source: https://www.celestica.com/blog/article/ai-infrastructure-for-the-data-center-and-beyond
