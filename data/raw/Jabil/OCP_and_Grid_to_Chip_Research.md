## Grid to Chip Infrastructure

### Power Management Solutions
1. **Integrated Power Solutions**
   - Modular, integrated power solutions enable power from **grid to chip**
   - Reduces voltage conversion losses and improves power efficiency across data center operations
   - Includes: Power Distribution Units (PDUs), Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems, low/medium-voltage switchgear
   - System-level power distribution modeling and testing
   - High-voltage design readiness to support evolving grid interfaces
   - Source: https://jabil.com/industries/data-center/data-center-infrastructure.html

### Rack Integration Capabilities
1. **System-Level Integration**
   - Engineers and builds entire rack-level systems: compute, storage, switching, photonics, power, and cooling
   - Design and manufacturing for AI, high-performance compute, and storage-class servers
   - System-level test and validation at both server and rack level
   - Regionalized supply chain management

### Liquid Cooling Solutions
1. **Advanced Cooling Technologies**
   - Acquired **Mikros Technologies** (October 2024) - industry leader in microchannel cold plate solutions
   - Cold plate design for high-wattage compute and AI workloads
   - Integrated CDU (Coolant Distribution Units) and rack plumbing solutions
   - Flexible compatibility with air, liquid, and hybrid cooling systems
   - Liquid cooled switch integration for networking and AI
   - Source: https://jabil.com/industries/data-center/data-center-infrastructure.html
