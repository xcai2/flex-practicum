# Benchmark Electronics - OCP and Grid to Chip Infrastructure Research

## OCP (Open Compute Project) Involvement

### Limited Direct OCP Engagement
Based on available information, Benchmark Electronics has **limited direct involvement** with the Open Compute Project compared to competitors like Jabil and Celestica. The company does not appear to have OCP-accepted products or be a major contributor to OCP specifications.

### Indirect OCP Connections
1. **Partnership with Oxide Computer**
   - Collaborative partnership in cloud computing hardware development
   - Oxide Computer is known for building OCP-inspired server racks
   - Benchmark provides manufacturing and engineering support for Oxide's innovative server rack designs
   - Partnership driven by shared values and technical expertise
   - Source: https://www.bench.com/setting-the-benchmark/a-collaborative-partnership-in-cloud-computing-hardware

2. **HPC/ML Benchmarks for OCP Servers**
   - YouTube presentation: "HPC/ML Benchmarks and Extensions for OCP Servers" (3 years ago)
   - Indicates some level of engagement with OCP ecosystem for performance testing
   - Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TDWhwRgAWjk
