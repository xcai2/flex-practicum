# Benchmark Reports Third Quarter 2025 Results - Enterprise AI Opportunities Ramping

**Date:** November 4, 2025  
**Type:** Earnings Release / Financial Results  
**Source:** https://ir.bench.com/news/news-details/2025/Benchmark-Reports-Third-Quarter-2025-Results/default.aspx

**Tags:** earnings, AI, enterprise AI, advanced computing, financial results, growth outlook

**CapEx Related:** No  
**AI Related:** Yes

---

## Summary

Benchmark reported Q3 2025 revenue of $681 million (up 3.5% YoY) and non-GAAP EPS of $0.62, achieving the high end of guidance. CEO Jeff Benck highlighted "the beginning stages of our ramping enterprise AI opportunities" as a key growth driver, with strong bookings momentum positioning the company for continued growth in 2026 and beyond.

---

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q3 2025 Results

**Revenue and Profitability:**
- Revenue: $681 million (up 3.5% YoY, up 6% sequentially)
- GAAP Operating Income: $24 million
- Non-GAAP Operating Income: $33 million
- Diluted GAAP EPS: $0.39
- Diluted non-GAAP EPS: $0.62

### Q4 2025 Guidance

- Revenue: $670 million - $720 million
- Diluted GAAP EPS: $0.44 - $0.50
- Diluted non-GAAP EPS: $0.62 - $0.68

## Key Leadership Quotes

**Jeff Benck, CEO:**

During the quarter we saw improved year-over-year performance across the majority of our market sectors and I am encouraged by indicators pointing to stronger growth as we exit the year, **including the beginning stages of our ramping enterprise AI opportunities**."
