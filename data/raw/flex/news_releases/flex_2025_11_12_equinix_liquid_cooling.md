## Summary

Flex deployed its advanced rack-level, vertically integrated liquid cooling solution at the Equinix Co-Innovation Facility (CIF) in Ashburn, Virginia. The deployment showcases standalone and facility-integrated single-phase direct liquid cooling (DLC) capabilities from JetCool (a Flex company) in a single Open Compute Project (OCP) ORv3 rack. The solution significantly reduces data center water and power consumption in AI and high-performance computing (HPC) environments, demonstrating Flex's end-to-end capabilities to solve power, heat, and scale challenges.

## Deployment Details

### Equinix Co-Innovation Facility (CIF)

**Location:** Ashburn, Virginia (major data center hub)

**Purpose:**
- Collaborative environment for testing emerging infrastructure technologies
- Real-world, high-density compute environment demonstrations
- Partner and customer evaluation of new cooling solutions
- Performance metrics and technical information showcase

**Flex Presence:**
- Over one year of presence at CIF
- Longstanding collaboration with Equinix on power and cooling infrastructure
- Products deployed across Equinix global facilities
- Newest consolidated demonstration of liquid cooling capabilities

### Integrated Liquid Cooling Solution

**Complete Rack-Level System:**
- Single OCP ORv3 rack with integrated liquid cooling
- Entire system manufactured by Flex (rack, cooling, power)
- Vertically integrated solution from single supplier
- End-to-end lifecycle support and warranty coverage

**Key Components:**

**1. JetCool SmartPlate System for Dell PowerEdge R760 Servers**
- Self-contained, closed-loop cooling system
- No external CDUs or facility plumbing required
- Minimal disruption transition from air to liquid cooling
- **15% reduction in total IT power consumption**
- Maximizes compute in power-constrained environments

**2. DLC Capabilities for Dell PowerEdge R660 Systems**
- JetCool cold plates for direct liquid cooling
- Operates with very warm coolant inlet temperatures (up to 70째C)
- **Up to 90% reduction in water usage** vs. other liquid cooling
- **Up to 50% reduction in cooling power consumption**
- Reliable performance at extreme compute densities
- Cools up to 4kW per processor socket with headroom for higher loads

**3. SmartSense 6U Liquid-to-Liquid (L2L) CDU**
- Facility-ready 6U rackmount unit
- Supports up to **300kW** of high-density DLC cooling
- Interfaces directly with Equinix TCS secondary loop
- Enables full DLC operation in facility-integrated mode

**4. Additional Integration Components**
- Manifolds for coolant distribution
- Busbars for power distribution
- Quick disconnect solutions for maintenance
- All integrated into single ORv3 rack
