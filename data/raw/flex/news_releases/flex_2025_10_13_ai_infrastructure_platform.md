## Summary

Flex announced the industry's first globally manufactured, fully integrated platform designed for gigawatt data centers supporting AI and HPC. By uniting power, cooling, compute, and global services into pre-engineered, modular reference designs, the platform enables operators to deploy infrastructure up to 30% faster, reduce execution risk, and scale reliably to meet the pace of AI demand.

## Breakthrough Product Innovations

### 1. 1MW Racks

**High-density liquid-cooled infrastructure:**
- **IT Rack:** Liquid-cooled for high-power AI servers
- **OCP-Inspired Power Rack:** Designed for +/-400V
- **800VDC Transition:** Enables next-generation power architectures
- **1MW Capacity:** Supports extreme power density AI workloads

**Significance:**
- Traditional racks: 5-10 kW
- AI racks (air-cooled): 20-40 kW
- Flex 1MW racks: 1,000 kW (100x traditional, 25x+ current AI)
- Enables gigawatt-scale data centers

### 2. Capacitive Energy Storage System

**Industry-first UL 1973-certified:**
- **First-to-market** capacitor energy storage system
- **UL 1973 certified** for safety and reliability
- **Reduces electrical disturbances** from AI workloads
- **Alternative to batteries** for short-duration energy storage

**AI Workload Benefits:**
- AI training creates power spikes and transients
- Capacitive storage smooths power delivery
- Protects grid and data center infrastructure
- Improves power quality and reliability

### 3. Modular Rack-Level CDU

**Scalable coolant distribution:**
- **Up to 1.8 MW capacity** per CDU
- **Flexible and scalable** for evolving workloads
- **AI, HPC, and hyperscale** workload support
- **Global support and warranty** coverage

**Cooling Capabilities:**
- Supports liquid cooling for high-power AI servers
- Modular design enables incremental scaling
- Rack-level distribution for efficiency
- Comprehensive monitoring and control

### 4. Prefabricated Power Pods and Skids

**Modular power infrastructure:**
- **Pre-engineered modular systems** simplify installation
- **Offsite assembly** reduces onsite labor
- **Parallel construction** shortens build times
- **Fewer interconnects** reduce complexity and risk

**Benefits:**
- Cuts weeks from construction schedules
