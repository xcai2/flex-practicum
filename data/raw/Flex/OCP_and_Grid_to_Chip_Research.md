### Vertical Power Delivery (VPD)

1. **VPD Technology**
   - Places voltage regulators directly beneath high-performance processors
   - Shortens power transmission path
   - Minimizes power plane resistance
   - Enhances current density and dramatically lowers power losses
   - Direct underside connections to processors and ASIC power pins
   - Manages power for GPUs in data center AI applications (1000A+ with ultra-fast load transient response)
   - Source: https://flexpowermodules.com/from-grid-to-chip-optimising-power-for-ai-data-centers
   - Source: https://flexpowermodules.com/vertical-power-delivery-solutions
