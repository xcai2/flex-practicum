# Flex Reports Second Quarter Fiscal 2026 Results - Strong Data Center Growth

**Date:** October 29, 2025  
**Type:** Earnings Release / Financial Results  
**Source:** https://investors.flex.com/financials/quarterly-results/default.aspx

**Tags:** earnings, data center growth, AI infrastructure, financial results, power and cloud  
**Relevance:** VERY HIGH - Record Q2 with strong data center demand  
**CapEx Related:** No  
**AI Related:** Yes

---

## Summary

Flex reported record Q2 FY2026 results with net sales up 4% at the top end of guidance, driven by strong data center demand in Power and Cloud businesses. The company raised full-year guidance for net sales, adjusted operating margin, and adjusted EPS. Flex delivered adjusted operating margin of 6.0%, marking the fourth consecutive quarter at or above 6%, demonstrating disciplined execution and strong positioning in the AI era.

---

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q2 FY2026 Results (Quarter Ended September 26, 2025)

**GAAP Results:**
- **Net Sales:** $6.8 billion (up 4%, at top end of guidance)
- **GAAP Operating Margin:** 4.4%
- **GAAP Operating Income:** $296 million
- **GAAP Net Income:** $199 million
- **GAAP EPS:** $0.52 (includes $41M Ukraine facility costs)
- **Cash Provided by Operating Activities:** $453 million

**Non-GAAP Results:**
- **Adjusted Operating Income:** $409 million
- **Adjusted Operating Margin:** 6.0% (fourth consecutive quarter at/above 6%)
- **Adjusted Net Income:** $300 million
- **Adjusted EPS:** $0.79 (record adjusted EPS)

**Performance vs. Guidance:**
- Net sales at top end of guidance
- Adjusted operating margin and adjusted EPS exceeded guidance
- Strong execution across business segments
