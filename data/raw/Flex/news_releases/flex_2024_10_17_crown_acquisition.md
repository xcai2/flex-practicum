# Flex to Acquire Crown Technical Systems for $325 Million

**Date:** October 17, 2024  
**Type:** Acquisition Announcement  
**Source:** https://investors.flex.com/news/news-details/2024/Flex-to-Acquire-Crown-Technical-Systems/default.aspx

**Tags:** acquisition, Crown Technical Systems, power distribution, data center, utility power, strategic investment  
**Relevance:** VERY HIGH - $325M strategic acquisition for data center power capabilities  
**CapEx Related:** Yes ($325M acquisition investment)  
**AI Related:** Yes (data center power infrastructure for AI)

---

## Summary

Flex announced the acquisition of Crown Technical Systems, a leader in fully integrated power distribution and protection systems, for $325 million in an all-cash transaction. Crown brings nearly three decades of expertise in modular solutions, medium voltage switchgear, and power control products, strengthening Flex's position to help customers solve power, heat, and scale challenges in the data center space. The transaction is expected to be accretive in the first year after closure.
